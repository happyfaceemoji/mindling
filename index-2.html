What is this?

It's a real neural network

Every Mindling is a small character-level net training live in your browser. Nothing is scripted — the babble is genuinely what the model predicts, one letter at a time.

It learns you

It only ever learns from what you type to it. Two people grow completely different creatures. It even hatches into a shape and colour seeded by your own first words.

It has a life

It begins as an egg, hatches once it's learned enough, and grows sharper the more you raise it. Come back tomorrow — it remembers everything you taught it.

How to raise it

FEED

Give it your words. It trains on every letter — the more you talk to it, the more it sounds like language, then like you.

REST

Let it sleep. It consolidates what it's heard so far and wakes up sharper. Use it to help a young one find its voice faster.

PLAY

Loosen its tongue. Cranks how adventurous its speech is, so it says stranger, more surprising things.
